Bar Ingredients

Bar Ingredients puts the world of spirits and cocktail ingredients in your pocket! Well, sort of. To be precise, it puts one specific interpretation of that world in your pocket!

This free app is a reference version of the MixologyTech Ingredients Database featuring thousands of spirits in an innovative organizational scheme. You can search the database or drill into it through the unique ingredient hierarchy. Or by flavor or country. You can even submit your own snapshots of ingredients to enliven the database.

When Internet service is available, the Bar Ingredients app will automatically update itself with the latest database entries.

iPad users: this app has no native iPad interface, but it is usable with the iPhone UI on iPad and iPad 2.

Please see also our related premium app My Bar.
