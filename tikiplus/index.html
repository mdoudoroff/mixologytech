FAQ

Who is Beachbum Berry?
Jeff “Beachbum” Berry is the de facto authority on Tiki cocktail and “Polynesian Pop” exotic drink history. Jeff is the author of Sippin’ Safari, Beachbum Berry Remixed, Beachbum Berry’s Taboo Table and other books that have literally resurrected exotic drinks for the current day. Dig deeper over at beachbumberry.com.
