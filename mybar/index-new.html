My Bar

My Bar is a premium iPhone app that makes inventory management feasible for liquor enthusiasts and mixologists.

My Bar does things that no other app can.

My Bar is not for everyone.

Please do not purchase My Bar until you know it will provide you with value.

Before purchasing My Bar:

  1. Download and play with our free app Bar Ingredients, which will introduce you to the MixologyTech Ingredients Database.
  2. Watch the demo video (below) to understand how My Bar’s features work.
  3. Email us with any questions or doubts you have.

Thank you!

—Martin Doudoroff (developer)
