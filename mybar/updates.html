My Bar Database Updates

The My Bar database is a living, growing database of entries about different bar ingredients, primarily liquor. The database evolves through ongoing work by the librarian, but especially from user submissions of barcodes, photos and such. Updates are distributed to My Bar users frequently

In this time series, red represents the total number of database entries (proprietary, generic, and abstract), and green represents the total number of barcodes. Each tick is an update.
