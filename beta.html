Beta Testing

We’re looking for a few patient folks to test a major new feature of our recipe apps (some other, smaller stuff is also included). As a tester, you would install and use the beta software in place of the officially released versions. If you see anything wrong, your job would be tell us about it, preferably including unambiguous steps to reproduce the problem—reporting a bug could take a little time and work on your part.

The major new feature you would be testing handles the syncing of Favorite and Flagged recipes, but expands that concept with the addition of ad hoc custom lists you can create, and by making all these lists cross-app in addition to being cross-device.

Prerequisites: in addition to the patience required to tolerate unfinished software and forward us bug reports, you need to already own at least two of the apps in our suite and you need to already have a sync account with us. It would be good if you were also using the apps on more than one device, but this is not required.

If you’re interested, please email us and we’ll talk. info@mixologytech.com.
