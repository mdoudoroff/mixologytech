PDT Cocktails

PDT Cocktails is an app for the iPhone and iPad. The product is published by Martin Doudoroff LLC. It costs US$9.99 and is sold exclusively through the App Store. The software and ingredient database was designed and developed by Martin Doudoroff. The content, consisting of four hundred drink recipes and another sixty-odd PDT ingredient recipes, plus all photography and related information is copyright Jim Meehan and published in the app under license. Each and every photo in the app was taken by PDT bartender Nick Brown.
