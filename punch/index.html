Wondrich’s Index of Punch is a practical reference to the essential punch bowl formulas of the past and present.

  • 64 recipes of hot and cold punches spanning six centuries (full list of included recipes by name)
  • Endless opportunities for variation
  • The ideal companion to David Wondrich’s seminal book Punch: The Delights (and Dangers) of the Flowing Bowl and our historical cocktail app, Martin’s Index of Cocktails
  • Check off the ingredients you’ve got and this app will show you which drinks you can make!
  • Filter the recipes on multiple criteria with just a few taps
  • Track favorite and flagged recipes
  • Ingredient definitions included
  • Works the same as our well-recieved apps, PDT Cocktails and Beachbum Berry’s Total Tiki
  • Universal app, works nearly identically on both platforms
