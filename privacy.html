MixologyTech Privacy Policy

Let’s keep this simple.

  • Our iPhone/iPad apps and our web apps don’t automatically collect any information about you on our behalf (or for any third party). Nobody should be tracking you through our stuff.
  • If you create an account with any of the services we operate, you are supplying an email address connected to you that serves both as the unique ID for the account, and a means of communication with you. We don’t share this email address with third parties and we generally only contact you through that email address for administrative purposes, although we might send you a product-related announcement if it seems important enough to us. Moreover:
    • We take what we believe are reasonable measures to protect your email address and your related account data, but make no guarantees or promises about it.
    • You should not assume that any information you input into our services is truly private and you should certainly not input anything personally sensitive. We are not a bank, we are a cocktails and booze enthusiast operation.
    • In the course of using your service account, you will input information of your own—such as inventory data and composed notes—that will be transmitted to our servers and back. While we generally hold your sync data in private between you and us, we do anonymously aggregate aspects of your data for our own purposes and to enrich the information we deliver through our services. (An example of an anonymous aggregate: which recipes have the most “likes”?) We may also elect to share anonymous aggregates with our partners or the public at large.
  • Our web sites may use cookies for session management purposes, but you really should not encounter any third party cookies here.
  • If you have a paid subscription to our services, the subscription itself (your billing info) is handled by a third party: Stripe. They have their own privacy policy and you can view it here.

Questions? Please email info@mixologytech.com.
