“Will there be an Android version?”

No Android version is planned at this time.

We are aware that demand exists. However, we’ve researched both the marketplace and the cost of porting our products. The unavoidable conclusion—in the current climate—is that it’s unlikely products such as ours could recoup their Android development costs in a timely manner, if ever. We will continue to monitor the evolving situation.

If this surprises you, keep in mind that cocktail apps, despite the popularity of cocktails, are niche products. They will never sell in the millions of units, and even hundreds of thousands of sales is out of reach for most. Meanwhile, app development is quite expensive and the downward price pressure is absolutely crushing; as it is, our $10 products are generally perceived as expensive. In the end, it’s just arithmetic.

Things change and never say never, but for the forseeable future, your only option is to get an iOS device.

—Martin
