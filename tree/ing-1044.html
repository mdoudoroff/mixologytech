    vanilla-flavored liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, nut or bean-flavored /
    • vanilla-flavored liqueur

    description

    Vanilla-flavored liqueur is a generic category for sweetened and flavored spirits of various dilutions with vanilla bean flavoring.

    flavors
    sweet, vanilla, spice
    substitute
    simple syrup with added vanilla extract
    places (country/region of production)
    Netherlands, France, United States, Bahamas, Italy, Normandy (France)

    examples

    • Bols Vanilla Liqueur
    • Coeur du Breuil
    • crème de vanille
    • DeKuyper Thrilla Vanilla
    • Dr. McGillicuddy’s Intense Vanilla Schnapps
    • Gaetano Vanilla Schnapps
    • Nassau Royale
    • Navan
    • Tuaca
