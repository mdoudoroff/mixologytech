    curaçao, blue-colored

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored /

    • curaçao, blue-colored

    description

    Blue curaçao is white curaçao with blue food coloring added for visual effect.

    flavors
    orange, citrus, fruit, alcohol, sweet
    substitute
    curaçao or triple sec with blue food coloring
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, France, Netherlands, Canada, Australia, Kentucky (United States), Curaçao
