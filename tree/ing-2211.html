    grape brandy

    • brandies /
    • brandy, aged /
    • grape brandy

    description

    Grape brandy is a generic category for brandy distilled from fermented grapes and aged in wood barrels or other vessels.

    flavors
    brandy, wood
    substitute
    other aged grape brandy or other aged brandy
    places (country/region of production)
    Cognac (France), Armagnac (France), Spain, France, California (United States), Armenia, Japan, United States, Greece, Germany, Albania, Portugal, Mexico, South Africa, Oregon (United States), Kentucky (United States)
