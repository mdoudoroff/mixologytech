    banana

    • garnishes & solids /
    • fruit, fresh /
    • banana

    description

    Banana refers, as a garnish, to a slice of a ripe (but fresh) banana. Used also as a flavoring additive in extract form, and as a constituent of liqueurs, juices, and purees. Noted in mixed drinks for both flavor and texture. Prevalent in tropical punch-style drinks. It can be advisable to dip peeled banana in lemon juice or lime juice before using as a garnish.
