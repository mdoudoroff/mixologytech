    elderflower liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, flower-flavored /
    • elderflower liqueur

    description

    Elderflower liqueur is generic for sweetened and flavored spirits of various dilutions flavored with the blossoms of the elderberry.

    flavors
    floral, sweet, elderflower
    places (country/region of production)
    Germany, United States, France

    examples

    • Bitter Truth Elderflower Liqueur, The
    • Pircher Holunderblüten
    • pür likör Blossom Elderflower
    • St. Germain
    • Thatcher’s Elderflower
