    anisette

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, herbal or spice-flavored /
    • anisette

    synonyms

    anise-flavored liqueur, liqueur d'anis, anis

    description

    Anisette is a generic category for anise-flavored liqueur in neutral spirit base.

    flavors
    sweet, anise, spice
    substitute
    other anis-flavored liqueur; pastis
    places (country/region of production)
    Spain, Canada, United States, France

    examples

    • Anis del Mono
    • Anisette Marie Brizard
    • Hiram Walker Anisette
    • Mr. Boston Anisette
    • Ojen
    • Tres Castillos Anis Fino Escarchado
