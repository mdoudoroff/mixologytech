    Pash DEFUNCT

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored /
    • passion fruit liqueur /
    • Pash

    description

    Defunct proprietary passion fruit liqueur of British manufacture which, as with Curacaos, was apparently available in more than one color. Repeatedly cited as an ingredient in the Cafe Royal Cocktail Book of 1937.

    Passion fruit liqueur is a generic category for a liqueur of neutral spirit or brandy base with passion fruit flavoring and/or juice. A recent popular brand is Alizé.

    flavors
    tropical fruit, sweet, fruit, passion fruit
    substitute
    Other passion fruit liqueur
