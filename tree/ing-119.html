    • orange peel, grated

    synonyms

    orange zest

    description

    Grated orange peel is the bits of rind and copious essential oils scraped from the outer surface of a fresh orange, generally with a zester or grating tool; grated orange peel should not include pith (the white part) which is bitter and without flavor.

    flavors
    orange, citrus, sour, fruit, orange essence
    substitute
    other citrus fruit
