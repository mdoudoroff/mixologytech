    clove

    • garnishes & solids /
    • herbs & spices /
    • clove

    description

    Clove is the dried, unopened bud of the tropical evergreen clove tree; used either whole or ground as a drink additive for its flavoring in extract, syrup & liqueur forms. Also used as a garnish on the surface of drinks in the same manner. Madagascar is the main supplier of cloves.

    flavors
    clove, spice
    substitute
    allspice, cinnamon, or nutmeg
