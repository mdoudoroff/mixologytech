    orange wheel

    • garnishes & solids /
    • fruit, fresh /
    • citrus fruit /
    • orange /
    • orange wheel

    synonyms

    slice of orange

    description

    An orange wheel is a cross-sectional slice of an orange, usually between an eighth and a quarter inch thick, producing the visual appearance of a spoked wheel.

    Orange wheels are typically floated atop a drink, inserted down the side of a glass, or slit with a knife and slotted on the edge of a glass.

    A half orange wheel is a wheel bisected in two to produce a semicircular garnish.

    flavors
    orange, citrus, sour, fruit
    substitute
    other citrus fruit
