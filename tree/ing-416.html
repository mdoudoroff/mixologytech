    Canadian whiskey

    • whiskey /
    • Canadian whiskey

    description

    Canadian whiskey is a generic category for whiskey produced in Canada, traditionally predominately of rye, but today principally of other grains; sometimes as a single-malt, but most often as a blended whiskey. Crown Royal and Canadian Club are noted brands, though there are many.

    flavors
    grain, tannin, wood, oak, whiskey
    substitute
    other blended whiskey, other whiskey
    places (country/region of production)
    Canada, United States
