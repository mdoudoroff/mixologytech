    spring water (effervescent)

    • non-alcoholic mixers & dilutants /
    • water /
    • spring water (effervescent)

    synonyms

    mineral water

    description

    Spring water is water bottled at a natural spring, ideally with as little handling and adulteration as possible. “Mineral water” implies notable quantities of (nutritive or flavoring) dissolved minerals. May be (naturally) carbonated or not.

    substitute
    Filtered water or seltzer.
    places (country/region of production)
    Italy, France

    sub-branches

    • San Pellegrino (1)

    examples

    • Apollinaris water
    • Perrier
    • Vichy water
