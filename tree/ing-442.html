    Port

    • wine, fortified /
    • Port

    synonyms

    Porto

    description

    Port is generic for a specific style of aged Portuguese fortified wine used mainly in dessert cocktails.

    flavors
    sweet, acid, wine, fruit, spice
    substitute
    other port or Madeira (imperfect substitute)
    places (country/region of production)
    Portugal, Australia, California (United States), Spain, South Africa, Maryland (United States), Texas (United States)

    sub-branches
