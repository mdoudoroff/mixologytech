    créme de cacao, dark

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, nut or bean-flavored /
    • chocolate-flavored liqueur /
    • crème de cacao, white /
    • créme de cacao, dark

    description

    Dark crème de cacao is generic for liqueur with chocolate flavoring and caramel coloring in a neutral spirits base.

    flavors
    sweet, chocolate, nutty
    substitute
    other chocolate-flavored liqueur; coffee-flavored liqueur like Kahlua or Tia Maria (imperfect substitute)
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Netherlands, Canada, Australia, Italy, France
