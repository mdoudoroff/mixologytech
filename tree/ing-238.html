    crème de vanille DEFUNCT

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, nut or bean-flavored /
    • vanilla-flavored liqueur /
    • crème de vanille

    synonyms

    creme de vanilla

    description

    Crème de vanille is generic for French liqueur with vanilla flavoring. As a commercial category, this ingredient is largely defunct, although regional examples may exist.

    flavors
    sweet, vanilla, spice
    substitute
    Other vanilla-flavored liqueur, Tuaca, sweetened vanilla-flavored vodka
    places (country/region of production)
    France
