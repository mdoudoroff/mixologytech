    brandy, unaged

    • brandies
      • brandy, unaged

    description

    Unaged brandy is a generic category for distilled fermented fruit juice (wine). “Unaged” indicates the spirit is not typically aged in wood, although it may be stored in glass or another neutral container for significant time, and undergo a degree of change on its own (without additives).

    Note that producers of these styles of brandy do sometimes sell wood-aged versions of their distillates, but that aging is a peripheral variation/modification rather than an integral feature of these spirits.
