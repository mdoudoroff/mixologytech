    syrup, lemongrass-infused

    • non-alcoholic sweeteners /
    • syrup /
    • syrup, herbal or spice-flavored /
    • syrup, lemongrass-infused

    description

    Lemongrass syrup is generic for home-made syrup infused with fresh lemongrass. To make:

    Peel and slice the cores of two fresh lemongrass stalks. Place in a saucepan with 1 cup sugar and 1 cup water. Bring to a boil, stir-ring until sugar is dissolved. Lower heat, cover saucepan, and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove saucepan from heat and, keeping it covered, let sit for at least 2 hours before straining and bottling. Store it in the fridge.

    Note: for aesthetics and to help maintain a fresh flavor, it can be useful to stuff a few intact lemongrass stalks into the bottled syrup and leave them there.
