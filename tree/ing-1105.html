    mint sprig

    • garnishes & solids /
    • herbs & spices /
    • mint /
    • mint sprig

    description

    A sprig of mint is a stem of mint with 3 or more mint leaves growing out of it. Suffice to say 6 to 8 LEAVES of mint is appropriate for most drinks calling for mint sprigs or leaves (such as Juleps & Mojitos) but otherwise lacking a listed quantity.

    flavors
    peppermint, herbal, mint, spearmint

    examples

    • mint sprig poked through a slice of cucumber
    • mint sprig, frosted
