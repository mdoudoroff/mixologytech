    apple cider

    • non-alcoholic mixers & dilutants /
    • fruit juice /
    • apple cider

    synonyms

    sweet cider

    description

    Apple cider is generic for the juice of apples picked early in the season with a higher acid and lower sugar content—often unpasteurized and clarified to a lesser degree than apple juice per sé. All of this enhances the potential for fermentation (i.e., hard apple cider) in a manner not present in the juice. Note that differences between what is “apple juice” and what is “apple cider” varies widely by brand, and sometimes there is NO difference. An excellent cocktail/mixed drink/punch additive, more prevalent in years-past than now. The meaning of cider in the United Kingdom is different, referring to a fermented apple beverage, drunk like (and with a similar proof to) beer. See also cider and apple juice.
