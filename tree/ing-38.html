    peach bitters

    • aromatic bitters /
    • peach bitters

    description

    Peach bitters is an aromatic bitters consisting of concentrated bitter tincture of peach pits, spices, and spirits. Extinct after Prohibition until revived in the mid-1990s, as the result of a conversation between John Fee and Ted Haigh, by Fee Bros. (Peach bitters are not “fruit bitters” because they are flavored with peach pits, not peach flesh, and their flavor reflects that—they are more closely related to amaretto in flavor than to peach.)
