    description

    Citrus-flavored vodka is generic for vodka which, after distillation, is, by infusion, maceration, aging, or secondary distillations, imbued with natural or artificial citrus flavoring, mainly lemon. Noted brand is Absolut.

    flavors
    lemon, alcohol, citrus, fruity, lime
