    • vodka, citrus-flavored

    description

    Citrus-flavored vodka is generic for vodka which, after distillation, is, by infusion, maceration, aging, or secondary distillations, imbued with natural or artificial citrus flavoring, mainly lemon. Noted brand is Absolut.

    flavors
    fruit, lemon, alcohol, citrus, lime
    substitute
    other flavored vodka
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Sweden, Netherlands, England (United Kingdom), Poland, California (United States), Czech Republic, Denmark, France, Russia
