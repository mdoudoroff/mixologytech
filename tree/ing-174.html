    peach liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored /
    • peach liqueur

    synonyms

    peach-flavored brandy, peach cordial, liqueur de peche, creme de peche, peach schnapps

    description

    Peach liqueur is a generic category for neutral spirit- or brandy-based liqueur with natural and/or artificial peach flavoring

    flavors
    sweet, fruit, peach
    substitute
    other peach liqueur, or apricot liqueur (imperfect substitute)
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, France, Netherlands, Italy, Canada, Australia, Denmark, Kentucky (United States), Austria, Cognac (France), Germany, Switzerland, New York (United States)
