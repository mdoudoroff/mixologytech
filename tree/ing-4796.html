    Jim Beam Devil’s Cut

    • whiskey /
    • Bourbon whiskey /
    • Jim Beam Devil’s Cut

    description

    Novel proprietary bourbon blending of six year old bourbon with whiskey that has been metaphorically wrung out of the wood of empty bourbon barrels (the means of extraction remain secret).

    Bourbon whiskey is a generic category for U.S. single-malt whiskey distilled from predominately corn mash with wheat or rye grains. Popular in all manner of mixed drinks 19th century-present.
