    grape

    • garnishes & solids /
    • fruit, fresh /
    • grape

    description

    Grapes refers to the fresh fruit of the grape vine, available in various varieties. Typically, as a garnish, one or more fresh grapes, with or without seeds and stems. Grapes are used most often in bowl drinks such as punches. Also the basis for juice, jam, spirits, and liqueurs used in drinks.
