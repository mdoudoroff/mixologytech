              • El Dorado Superior High Strength Rum 151

    description

    Filtered, overproof Demerara rum. While technically a 151-proof Demerara rum, this product is filtered, uncolored, and scant on flavor or aromatics. Its purpose beyond lighting things on fire is unclear.

    151-proof Demerara rum is generic for overproof rum produced in Guyana in both white (filtered) and dark (caramel-colored) styles.

    flavors

    molasses, rum
