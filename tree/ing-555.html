    Scotch-flavored liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, spirit-flavored /
    • Scotch-flavored liqueur

    description

    Scotch-flavored liqueur is generic for liqueurs produced in Scotland with a Scotch whisky base—and flavor. Examples are Drambuie and Grand Tully.

    flavors
    sweet, smoky, whiskey
    substitute
    other scotch-flavored liqueur; non-cream-based irish whiskey-flavored liqueur or swedish punch (imperfect substitutes)
    places (country/region of production)
    Scotland, Speyside Scotch Region (Scotland)

    sub-branches

    • Scotch-flavored cream-based liqueur (3)

    examples

    • Drambuie
    • Drambuie 15 Year Old
    • Famous Grouse Liqueur
    • Glayva
    • Macallan Amber Liqueur
    • Old Pulteney Malt Scotch Whisky Liqueur
