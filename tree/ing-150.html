    cherry liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored /
    • cherry liqueur

    synonyms

    cherry cordial, cherry brandy

    description

    Cherry liqueur is a generic category for liqueurs with natural and/or artificial cherry flavoring & coloring in either neutral spirit or grape brandy base.

    flavors
    berries, sweet, fruit, cherry
    substitute
    other cherry-flavored liqueur or crème de cassis or raspberry liqueur (imperfect substitutes)
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, France, Netherlands, Italy, Portugal, Denmark, Kentucky (United States), Australia, Canada, Colorado (United States), Cognac (France), Croatia, Switzerland, Germany, Bosnia-Herzegovina
