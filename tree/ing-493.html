    • vodka, orange-flavored

    description

    Orange-flavored vodka is generic for vodka which, after distillation, is, by infusion, maceration, aging, or secondary distillations, imbued with natural or artificial orange flavoring. Noted brands are Stoli Orang and Grey Goose orange vodka.

    flavors
    orange, citrus, fruity, alcohol
