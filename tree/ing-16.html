amer

  • liqueurs
    • aperitif/digestif bitters
      • amer

description

Amer is the generic term for intensely-flavored aperitif/digestif bitters of French origin. Similar to the Italian “Amaro”. Amer tend to be lower proof than their Italian counterparts, and tend to be more focused on the aperitif role than the digestif role. Domestic imitations in some markets.

flavors

herbal, bitter

substitute

Other aperitif/digestif bitters. Nearly all aperitif/digestif bitters are complex, proprietary formulas and even products that aim to knock off better known brands tend to noticeably distinct. Factoring the extreme flavor potency of all these liqueurs, none are exactly substitutes for each other, but othen one can be substituted to different effect.

origin

France

  • Amer Picon Bière
  • Amer Picon Club
  • Amère Nouvelle
  • Amère Sauvage
