Absolut

  • brands
    • Absolut

description

Swedish vodka brand that traces its history to 1879, although the brand “Absolut” as we know it was not introduced until 1979 as part of an untterly deliberate—and staggeringly successful—move to launch a new international vodka brand. Today, Absolut is one of the three largest spirit brands in the world by volume. As of this writing, Absolut is owned by Pernod Ricard.
