    • crème de menthe

    synonyms

    creme de mint, menthe

    description

    Crème de menthe is a generic category for liqueur with spearmint or peppermint flavoring in a neutral spirit base. Virtually all liqueur companies with broad product ranges produce a crème de menthe. Available in white (clear) & green-colored versions.

    flavors
    herbal, sweet, mint
    substitute

    Commentary

    Traditional crème de menthe is a straightforward liqueur, often sold in both a colorless and a lurid, artificially green-colored version. However, mixologists more interested in the flavor of their drinks than the color will seldom need to look beyond Get 31 and Branca Menta for their mint liqueur needs although neither is technically a creme de menthe. Get 31 is a superior, clear peppermint-flavored liqueur (also available in the green-colored Get 27 formulation). Branca Menta is a more complex mint and herb digestif from the makers of Fernet Branca. Both Get and Branca Menta often make preferable direct substitutes for crème de menthe.
