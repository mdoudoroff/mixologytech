crème de menthe

  • liqueurs
    • liqueurs, herbal or spice-flavored
      • mint-flavored liqueur or schnapps
        • crème de menthe

synonyms

creme de mint, menthe

description

Crème de menthe is a generic category for liqueur with spearmint or peppermint flavoring in a neutral spirit base. Virtually all liqueur companies with broad product ranges produce a crème de menthe. Available in white (clear) & green-colored versions.

flavors

herbal, mint

substitute

peppermint schnapps (imperfect substitute)

origin
