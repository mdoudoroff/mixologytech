    port, ruby

    • fermented & brewed alcoholic ingredients /

    • Port /
    • port, ruby

    description

    Ruby port is generic for a class of port wine generally not vintage-specific, and aged in glass rather than wood, preserving the lighter coloration that gives it its name. Ruby port is typically less expensive than vintage port and commonly used in cocktails.

    flavors
    sweet, spice, acid, fruit, wine
    substitute
    other port or Madeira (imperfect substitute)
    places (country/region of production)
    Portugal, Maryland (United States), South Africa
