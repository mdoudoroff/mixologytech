    raspberry syrup

    • non-alcoholic sweeteners /
    • syrup /
    • syrup, fruit-flavored /
    • raspberry syrup

    description

    Raspberry syrup is a preparation of concentrated raspberry juice and sugar syrup. Along with red currant syrup (which see), often used as a substitute for grenadine (which see), as well as frequently in its own right in drinks, mainly previous to 1950.

    flavors
    sweet, raspberry, fruit, berries
    substitute
    grenadine or groseille syrup
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Pennsylvania (United States), France, Austria, Germany, California (United States)
