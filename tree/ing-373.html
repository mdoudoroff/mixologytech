    Tabasco sauce

    • non-beverage concentrates /

    • Tabasco sauce

    description

    Proprietary Louisiana hot sauce of aged red peppers, salt and distilled high-grain vinegar aged in white oak barrels for 3 years. A classic ingredient of the Bloody Mary and related drink forms.Created, produced, and owned by McIlhenny Company, Avery Island, Louisiana since 1868.

    Hot sauce is a generic category for condiment sauces made from chile peppers and other ingredients, usually in a vinegar base. Sauces vary extensively in ingredients, concentration, flavor, color and heat.
