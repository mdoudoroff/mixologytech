    coffee-flavored liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, nut or bean-flavored /
    • coffee-flavored liqueur

    synonyms

    creme de kaffee

    description

    Coffee-flavored liqueur is a generic category for liqueur containing natural and/or artificial coffee flavoring in neutral spirit or rum base and sweetener. Examples are Kahlua, Tia Maria, and creme de café, and creme de moka.

    flavors
    sweet, coffee, chocolate, nutty
    substitute
    Kahlua, Tia Maria, or domestic imitations of Kahlua
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Mexico, Italy, Netherlands, Canada, Australia, Colorado (United States), Denmark, Costa Rica, Oregon (United States), Guatemala, California (United States), Belgium, Venezuela

    sub-branches

    • crème de moka (1)
