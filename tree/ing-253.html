    Swedish Punch/Punsch

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, spirit-flavored /
    • Swedish Punch/Punsch

    synonyms

    Arrack Punsch, Caloric Punch, Arrack Punch, Caloric Punsch, Arak Punch, Arak Punsch

    description

    Swedish Punch is generic for a category of smoky Scandinavian liqueur with a base of Batavia Arak. Batavia Arak is a distillate of fermented sugar cane juice and fermented Javanese rice—a sharp, smoky, rummy spirit. Swedish Punsch (or Punch) sweetens this with cane sugar to produce an unforgettable liqueur. Notable brands are Cederlund’s, Carlshamn’s and Gronstedt. Like a dark smoky rum and when added to other lesser rums tends to enhance their value in an estimable way.

    flavors
    sweet, smoky, rum
    substitute
    other rum flavored liqueur, or home-made; Haus Alpenz provides the following infusion recipe: 180 ml Batavia Arrack, 100 ml strong brewed tea (black), 135 g sugar, 3/4 tsp vanilla extract, lemon peel, fresh ground cardamom.
    places (country/region of production)
    Sweden, Germany

    examples

    • Carlshamns Flaggpunsch
    • Cederlund’s Caloric Punch
    • Forgotten Flavours Swedish Punch
    • J. D. Grönstedt & Co. Punsch
    • Kronan Swedish Punsch
    • Strands Punsch

    Affinities

    Commentary

    As Ted “Dr. Cocktail” Haigh has observed repeatedly, a tiny quantity (1/2 tsp or 1 tsp) of Swedish Punsch added to an inexpensive brown rum in a mixed drink can impart some of the additional complexity one expects from more heavily-aged rums. This is a handy trick that should only be employed toward honest and ethical ends.
