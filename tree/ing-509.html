    blackberry liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored /
    • blackberry liqueur

    synonyms

    blackberry-flavored brandy, blackberry schnapps

    description

    Blackberry liqueur is generic for liqueur with natural and/or artificial blackberry flavoring and coloring in neutral spirit or grape brandy base. A noted brand is Echte Kroatzbeere from Germany.

    flavors
    sweet, fruit, blackberry, berries
    substitute
    crème de cassis or raspberry liqueur (imperfect substitutes)
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, France, Netherlands, Europe, Germany, Canada, Italy, Colorado (United States), Poland
