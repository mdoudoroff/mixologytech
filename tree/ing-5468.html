    Clément Rhum Vieux X.O.

    • sugar cane & molasses-based spirits
      • rum
        • rhum agricole
          • rum, Martinique
            • Clément Rhum Vieux X.O.

    description

    Rhum agricole aged at least three years in used whisky and bourbon barrels.

    Martinique rum is generic for the rums of the island of Martinique which have a dry complex vegetal character and which, today, enjoy A.O.C. designation. Martinique rums are “rhum agricole”—rum produced from fermented sugar cane juice, as opposed to fermented molasses.

    While the native spirit is an unaged, white rum, typically bottled at 100-proof, most distilleries also produce aged rums primarily for export.

    flavors

    vegetal, sugarcane juice, rum

    substitute

    dryer Jamaican dark, Haitian rum
