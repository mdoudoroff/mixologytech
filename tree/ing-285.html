    • cayenne pepper

    description

    Cayenne pepper is a powdered condiment of a specific variety of ground dried hot red pepper. Cocktail use, usually in conjunction with drinks that are either Bloody Mary variants or contain egg, such as the MAE WEST COCKTAIL.

    piquant, chile pepper, vegetal
