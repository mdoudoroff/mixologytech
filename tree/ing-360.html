    • Schweppes Bitter Lemon

    description

    Proprietary tart sparkling lemon mixer/soft drink with quinine from England, created in 1957 by Cadbury Schweppes. Of limited availability, but highly prized in mixed drinks.

    Lemon soda is any sweetened, carbonated water beverage with natural or artificial lemon flavoring.
