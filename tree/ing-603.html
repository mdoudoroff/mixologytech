    • Bacardi 8 Años

    description

    Proprietary bottling of Bacardi rum. Completely different than any other Bacardi rum on the market. Despite the name, this is probably not an 8 year old rum in any orthodox sense, but a blend of aged rums.

    Molasses-based rum is a generic category for rum distilled from fermented molasses.
