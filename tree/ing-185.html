triple sec

  • liqueurs
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored
      • orange-flavored liqueur
        • triple sec

description

Triple sec is a generic category for neutral spirit-based refined colorless orange-flavored liqueur imitative of Cointreau. 19th century bar guides often used the term “white curacao” to refer to it.

flavors

orange, citrus, fruity

substitute

other triple sec, other orange-flavored liqueur
