    allspice

    • garnishes & solids /
    • herbs & spices /
    • allspice

    synonyms

    pimiento berry

    description

    Allspice is a spice derived from the pepper berry more correctly known as pimiento or pimento. As a garnish, usually grated atop drinks hot or cold. Also used in punches, in liqueur form, and in some tropical drinks.

    flavors
    clove, nutmeg, cinnamon, spice
    substitute
    cinnamon, nutmeg, & clove in combination
    places (country/region of production)
    Jamaica
