    rum, Puerto Rican

    • sugar cane & molasses-based spirits /

    • rum, Puerto Rican

    synonyms

    light Puerto Rican rum, white Puerto Rican rum

    description

    Puerto Rican rum is generic for the light-bodied rums typical of Puerto Rico. Unless otherwise specified, a colorless or pale spirit is implied. Dark rums of various levels of quality are also made there, but for mixed drink use, they will be called for by name or additional description.
