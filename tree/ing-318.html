    guava nectar

    • non-alcoholic mixers & dilutants /
    • fruit juice /
    • guava nectar

    synonyms

    guava juice, guava purée, guava syrup

    description

    Guava nectar is a canned or bottled preparation of the juice and pulp of the sweet, acidic yellow tropical fruit, for which it is named, with sweetener. Used almost exclusively in tropical drinks. The plain juice of this fruit, either fresh or canned, is rarely seen.

    flavors
    tropical fruit, guava, fruit
