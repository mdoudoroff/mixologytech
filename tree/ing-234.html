    crème de cacao, white

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, nut or bean-flavored /
    • chocolate-flavored liqueur /
    • crème de cacao, white

    synonyms

    creme de cocoa

    description

    White crème de cacao is a generic for a colorless liqueur with chocolate flavoring in a neutral spirits base.

    flavors
    sweet, chocolate, nutty
    substitute
    other chocolate-flavored liqueur; coffee-flavored liqueur like Kahlua or Tia Maria (imperfect substitute)
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Netherlands, France, Canada, Australia, Italy, Kentucky (United States)

    sub-branches

    • créme de cacao, dark (16)
