    • La Favorite Vieux

    description

    La Favorite Vieux is a proprietary example of rhum agricole vieux.

    Rhum agricole vieux is generic for an aged rhum agricole, such as La Favorite Vieux. Technically, vieux rhum is supposed to be aged at least three years in barrels smaller than 650 liters.

    flavors
