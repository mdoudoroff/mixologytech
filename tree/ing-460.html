    • wine, Rhine

    description

    Rhine wine is generic for wine (predominantly white, usually a bit sweet, often fruity) produced in or near the Rhine river valley in Germany. Historically, wines produced elsewhere in this style were also christened with the name.

    flavors
    acid, fruit
    substitute
    other sweeter white wine
    places (country/region of production)
    Germany
