    Gosling’s Black Seal rum

    • Gosling’s Black Seal rum

    description

    Proprietary brand of dark/heavy-bodied, strong-flavored rum distilled from molasses in Bermuda. The family-owned Gosling Brothers Ltd. is the oldest business in Bermuda.

    Molasses-based rum is a generic category for rum distilled from fermented molasses.
