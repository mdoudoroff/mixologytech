    Champagne

    • fermented & brewed alcoholic ingredients /
    • wine, grape /
    • wine, sparkling /
    • Champagne

    description

    Champagne is generic for specific regional sparkling white or rosé wine from Champagne, France, popular since the mid-19th century in cocktails. Notable examples are the Champagne Cocktail, the Elysian Nymphs, and the French 75.

    acid, fruit
