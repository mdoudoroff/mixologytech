Irish cream

  • liqueurs
    • liqueurs, spirit-flavored
      • Irish whiskey liqueur
        • Irish cream

synonyms

Irish cream liqueur

description

Irish cream is generic for mixture of spirits, Irish whiskey, cream, spices, preservatives, and sweeteners. The Baileys brand is most famous.

flavors

irish whiskey, sweet, cream

substitute

No proper substitute

origin

Ireland
