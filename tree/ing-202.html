    ginger-flavored liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, herbal or spice-flavored /
    • ginger-flavored liqueur

    description

    Ginger-flavored liqueur is a generic category for liqueurs with a neutral spirit or brandy base and natural or artificial ginger flavoring.

    flavors
    sweet, ginger, spice
    substitute
    ginger wine
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Netherlands, France, China, Germany

    examples

    • DeKuyper Ginger
    • Domaine de Canton
    • Dracula Original
    • G. E. Massenez Crème de Gingembre
