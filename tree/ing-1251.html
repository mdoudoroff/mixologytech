    orange-flavored liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored /
    • orange-flavored liqueur

    description

    Orange-flavored liqueur is generic for various liqueur with a neutral or aged brandy base and natural or artificial orange flavoring.

    flavors
    orange, citrus, fruit, alcohol, sweet
