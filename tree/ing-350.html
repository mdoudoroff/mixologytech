    pineapple juice

    • non-alcoholic mixers & dilutants /
    • fruit juice /
    • pineapple juice

    description

    Pineapple juice is fresh-pressed or canned pineapple juice. Note: some commercial processed pineapple juice has extra sweetening; only use unsweetened pineapple juice in cocktail and mixed drink recipes unless they specify otherwise.

    flavors
    tropical fruit, fruit, pineapple
