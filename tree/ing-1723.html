    • non-alcoholic mixers & dilutants

    description

    Non-alcoholic mixers & dilutants is an abstract category for liquids whose primary function is to dilute a mixed drink; often these ingredients are actually the largest ingredient of the drink by volume, with the alcoholic components playing a flavoring role.
