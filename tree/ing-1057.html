    squeeze of lemon

    • garnishes & solids /
    • fruit, fresh /
    • citrus fruit /
    • lemon /
    • squeeze of lemon

    description

    A squeeze of lemon is, typically, a wedge cut from a fresh lemon—chopped in half if too large—that is served alongside the drink so the customer can squeeze the lemon into the drink themselves.

    Alternatively, the bartender performs the squeeze during service.

    flavors
    citrus, sour, lemon, fruit
    substitute
    other citrus fruit
