    Canadian Club

    • Canadian whiskey /
    • Canadian Club

    synonyms

    CC

    description

    Proprietary brand of Canadian whiskey of some acclaim. As with the majority of Canadian whiskies, Canadian Club is a blended whiskey - blended with grain neutral spirits. It is aged for 6 years. CC is notable in several tall drinks and in cocktails such as the Old Nick.

    Canadian whiskey is a generic category for whiskey produced in Canada, traditionally predominately of rye, but today principally of other grains; sometimes as a single-malt, but most often as a blended whiskey. Crown Royal and Canadian Club are noted brands, though there are many.
