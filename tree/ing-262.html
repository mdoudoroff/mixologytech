    Gordon’s Gin

    • neutral spirits & grain eau de vie /
    • gin /
    • gin, London dry /
    • Gordon’s Gin

    description

    Proprietary London dry gin. Notable in that it was specified in Ian Flemming's first James Bond cocktail/Martini, the Vesper. The American and British versions are different.

    London dry gin is generic for the de facto sharp, bone-dry style of gin that has dominated the market since the late 19th Century: a column-still distillate of grain with juniper and other botanicals. The vast bulk of all gins employed in cocktails are London dry gin. Gins identifying themselves as “dry gin” are imitative or sympathetic to the London Dry style. Beefeater London Dry Gin and Tanqueray London Dry Gin would be prominent definitive examples of the style, which encompasses legions of brands.
