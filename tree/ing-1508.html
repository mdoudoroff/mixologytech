Cruzan Single Barrel Estate Rum

          • Cruzan Single Barrel Estate Rum

description

Proprietary blend of 4- to 12-year aged rums that are—unusually—finished in new, charred American oak casks, the sort of wood employed with Bourbon and Straight Rye.

Virgin Islands rum is generic for the rums of Virgin Islands, most of which comes from St. Croix, once known as Santa Cruz. (St. Croix is the French equivalent of the Spanish Santa Cruz.) Contemporary rum from St. Croix is of light character. Cruzan (CREWZ-shun) is a noted producer. Unless otherwise specified, a colorless or pale spirit is implied.

flavors

rum

substitute

other light rum, such as from Puerto Rico or Cuba; nineteenth century (and earlier) recipes referencing Santa Cruz rum probably have in mind a pot still-type rum, in which case a rum from Barbados, Jamaica or Guyana might be a more appropriate substitute
