    Madeira

    • fermented & brewed alcoholic ingredients /
    • wine, grape /
    • wine, fortified /
    • Madeira

    description

    Madeira is generic for specific Portuguese sweet fortified wines from the island of Madeira.

    flavors
    sweet, acid, wine, fruit, spice
    substitute
    a sweeter port (imperfect substitute)
    places (country/region of production)
    Portugal, Madeira Islands, New York (United States)
