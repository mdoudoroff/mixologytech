    scotch whisky, blended malt

    • whiskey /
    • Scotch whisky /
    • scotch whisky, blended malt

    synonyms

    vatted scotch whisky

    description

    Vatted scotch whisky—now termed “Blended Malt Scotch Whisky” by law—is a generic category for a combination of single malts (from different distilleries.) Similar to what in America is called a “blend of straight whiskies”.

    flavors
    whiskey, wood, grain, scotch, smoky, oak, tannin
    substitute
    other vatted or single-malt scotch whisky
    places (country/region of production)
    Scotland (United Kingdom), United Kingdom
