    Pura Vida Gold

    • succulent-based spirits
      • mescal
        • tequila
          • tequila agave (100%)
            • tequila reposado (100%)
              • Pura Vida Gold

    description

    In spite of the bizarre use of the word “gold” on the label, this is a 100% agave reposado.

    Tequila reposado is generic for distilled agave spirit from Jalisco, Mexico that has been aged 60 days to a year in wood.

    flavors

    herbal, vegetal, smoky, agave

    substitute

    other Tequila reposado or a white Tequila

    abv

    40.0%

    origin

    Mexico
