    crème de rose DEFUNCT

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, flower-flavored /
    • Rose petal flavored liqueur /
    • crème de rose

    description

    Crème de rose is generic for a largely defunct liqueur with natural and/or artificial rose flower flavoring/coloring and a neutral spirit base.

    flavors
    floral, sweet, rose
    substitute
    Rosolio, or rose water, simple syrup, and scant vodka
    places (country/region of production)
    France

    examples

    • Vedrenne Creme de Rose
