    brown sugar

    • garnishes & solids /
    • sugar /
    • brown sugar

    description

    Brown sugar is a mixture of refined cane sugar & molasses, also from cane. The lighter the sugar, the lighter the flavor. Occasionally found in mixed drinks—mainly in the whiskey and rum families—and in hot, dessert-style beverages.

    flavors
    sweet, molasses
    substitute
    cane syrup, honey, agave syrup
