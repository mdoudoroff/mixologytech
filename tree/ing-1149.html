    description

    Aged brown rum from Jamaica with or without caramel coloring. Jamaican rums are heavy bodied and flavorful.

    flavors
    pungent, rum
    substitute
    other dark rum, sweeter, richer
    places (country/region of production)
    Jamaica, Puerto Rico [United States]
