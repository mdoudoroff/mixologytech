    • Navan

    description

    Proprietary, aged vanilla liqueur with a Cognac base. Navan is flavored with real vanilla beans from Madagascar and manufactured alongside Grand Marnier.

    Vanilla-flavored liqueur is a generic category for sweetened and flavored spirits of various dilutions with vanilla bean flavoring.

    flavors
    sweet, vanilla, spice
    substitute
    simple syrup with added vanilla extract
