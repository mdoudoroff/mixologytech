description

Bushmills 10 Year Single Malt is a proprietary example of Irish whiskey.

Irish whiskey is a generic category for whiskey made in Ireland, either blended or single-malt. The vast bulk of “Irish whiskey” is blended, with most malts and single malts labeled as such or as “pure pot still”.
