    crème de menthe, green

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, herbal or spice-flavored /
    • mint-flavored liqueur or schnapps /
    • crème de menthe /
    • crème de menthe, green

    description

    Green crème de menthe is generic for liqueurs with spearmint or peppermint flavoring and natural or artificial green coloring in a neutral spirit base.

    flavors
    herbal, sweet, mint
    substitute
    peppermint schnapps (imperfect substitute) and food coloring.
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Canada, Australia, Kentucky (United States), France, Netherlands

    examples

    • DeKuyper Creme de Menthe, green
    • Drillaud Green Mint
    • Du Bouchett Crème de Menthe (green)
    • Hiram Walker Creme de Menthe (green)
    • Potter’s Creme de Menthe (green)
    • VOK Creme de Menthe
