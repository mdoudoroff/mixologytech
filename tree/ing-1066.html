    squeeze of lime

    • garnishes & solids /
    • fruit, fresh /
    • citrus fruit /
    • lime /
    • squeeze of lime

    description

    A squeeze of lime is, typically, a wedge of lime—chopped in half if too large—that is served alongside the drink so the customer can squeeze the juice into the drink themselves.

    Alternatively, the bartender performs the squeeze during service.

    flavors
    sour, citrus, fruit, lime
    substitute
    other fresh seasonal fruit (imperfect substitute).
