    sherry, cream

    • fermented & brewed alcoholic ingredients /
    • wine, grape /
    • wine, fortified /
    • sherry /
    • sherry, cream

    synonyms

    sweet sherry

    description

    Cream sherry is a generic category for the sweetest style of sherry that begins as one of the dry varieties and is sweetened with moscatel or Pedro Ximénez wine. (No dairy involved.)

    flavors
    acid, fruit
    substitute
    other sherry, or other fortified wine
    places (country/region of production)
    Spain, New York (United States)

    examples

    • Hartley & Gibson Diez y Ocho Cream Sherry
    • Harvey’s Bristol Cream
    • Lustau Cream Candela
    • Lustau Rare Cream “Superior”
    • Osborne Santa María Cream Sherry
    • Sandeman Armada Rich Cream Oloroso
    • Taylor Cream Sherry
    • Wisdom & Warter Cream Sherry
