    rum, Demerara

    • sugar cane & molasses-based spirits /
    • rum /
    • rum, molasses-based /
    • rum, Demerara

    synonyms

    Guyanian rum, Guyana rum, Guyanan rum

    description

    Demerara rum is generic for the distinctive heavy-bodied dark rums in various proofs from the country of Guyana. The name Demerara is inherited from the British colony of Demerara, now a region of Guyana.

    As of this writing, all distilling in Guayana is centralized under Demerara Distillers Ltd., so all Demerara rum today is produced at this distillery, regardless of brand.
