    kümmel

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, herbal or spice-flavored /
    • kümmel

    synonyms

    kimmel, kummel

    description

    Kümmel is generic for grain-neutral-spirit-based caraway-flavored liqueur from Northern Europe. Best-known brand example in the United States: Gilka Kummel.

    flavors
    sweet, caraway, spice
    substitute
    domestic imitations in some markets
    places (country/region of production)
    Germany, Scandinavia, France, United States

    examples

    • Combier Doppelt Kummel Extra
    • Getreide-Kümmel
    • Gilka kummel
    • Helbing Kümmel
    • Jacquin Kümmel
    • Joseph Cartron Kummel
