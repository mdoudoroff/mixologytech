    apple

    • garnishes & solids /
    • fruit, fresh /
    • apple

    description

    Apple refers—as a garnish—to a thin, sectioned wedge or skin paring, sliced from a fresh apple, either red or green, and placed either on or in the glass. Also used as a drink additive for its flavoring in juice, cider, syrup, pureed sauce, jellied, brandy, and liqueur forms.

    flavors
    fruit, apple
    substitute
