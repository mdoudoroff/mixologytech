    Heering Coffee Liqueur

    • liqueurs
      • liqueurs, nut or bean-flavored

          • Heering Coffee Liqueur

    description

    Heering Coffee Liqueur is a proprietary example of coffee-flavored liqueur.

    Coffee-flavored liqueur is a generic category for liqueur containing natural and/or artificial coffee flavoring in neutral spirit or rum base and sweetener. Examples are Kahlua, Tia Maria, and creme de café, and creme de moka.

    flavors

    cocoa, nutty, coffee, chocolate, spice

    substitute

    Kahlua, Tia Maria, or domestic imitations of Kahlua

    origin

    Denmark
