    nutmeg

    • garnishes & solids /
    • herbs & spices /
    • nutmeg

    description

    Nutmeg is the kernel of the fruit of the nutmeg tree, a hard-shelled seed. Typically employed via a few strokes shaved off a whole nutmeg with a nutmeg grinder tool, or shakes of pre-ground nutmeg over the surface of (mainly heated) drinks—both as flavoring and as garnish. Closely associated with punch.

    flavors
    nutmeg, spice
    substitute
    clove, cinnamon, allspice
