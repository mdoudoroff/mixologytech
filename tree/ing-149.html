    Cherry Heering

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored /
    • cherry liqueur /
    • Cherry Heering

    synonyms

    Peter Heering, Heering

    description

    Proprietary Danish cherry liqueur originally produced with an aged brandy base, now with a neutral spirits (or near-neutral) base. Produced since 1818 under various names such as "Heering," "Peter Heering," and "Cherry Heering." Highly valued in cocktails; considered the amongst the best of the dark cherry liqueurs.

    Cherry liqueur is a generic category for liqueurs with natural and/or artificial cherry flavoring & coloring in either neutral spirit or grape brandy base.

    flavors
    berries, sweet, fruit, cherry
    substitute
    Cherry Marnier or other cherry-flavored brandy, other cherry liqueur

    Commentary

    Pairs well with and can assimilate large quantities of bitters such as Peychaud’s. Note Jamie Boudreau’s Pax Sax Sarax: 2 oz Glenmorangie, 1/4 oz Peychaud’s bitters, 1/4 oz Cherry Heering; stir with ice, strain into absinthe-rinsed cocktail glass, garnish with 3 brandied cherries.
