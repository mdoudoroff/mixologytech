    cranberry juice

    • non-alcoholic mixers & dilutants /
    • fruit juice /
    • cranberry juice

    description

    Cranberry juice is the juice (commercially extracted) of a specific small acidic red berry, most noted as an ingredient of the Cosmopolitan.

    flavors
    fruit, cranberry, sweet, berries, bitter, tart
    substitute
    other cranberry juice; pomegranate juice (imperfect substitute)
    places (country/region of production)
    United States

    • cranberry juice, white
