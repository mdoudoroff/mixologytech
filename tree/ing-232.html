    chocolate-flavored liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, nut or bean-flavored /
    • chocolate-flavored liqueur

    description

    Chocolate-flavored liqueur is a generic category for liqueur with natural and/or artificial chocolate flavoring and a neutral spirit or brandy base. Cream or similar dairy product is added in several brands.

    flavors
    sweet, chocolate, nutty
    substitute
    other chocolate-flavored liqueur; coffee-flavored liqueur like Kahlua or Tia Maria (imperfect substitute)
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Netherlands, France, Austria, Belgium, Canada, Italy, Australia, Uruguay, California (United States)

    sub-branches

    • crème de cacao, white (35)
