    passion fruit syrup

    • non-alcoholic sweeteners /
    • syrup /
    • syrup, fruit-flavored /
    • passion fruit syrup

    synonyms

    Passionola (defunct early brand)

    description

    Passion fruit syrup is a sweetened, passion fruit-derived syrup in several colors (the coloration does not affecting the flavor). Used almost exclusively in tropical punch-like drinks.

    flavors
    tropical fruit, sweet, fruit, passion fruit
    substitute
    other passion fruit syrup
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, France, California (United States), Oregon (United States)
