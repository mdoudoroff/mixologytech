    whisky, Japanese

    • whiskey
      • whisky, Japanese

    description

    Japanese whisky is generic for whisky made in Japan, either blended or single-malt, in a style which seems to cross Irish and Scotch whiskies with a bolt of dry American rye thrown in for good measure. Suntory is a major producer. The quality of this product tends to be high.

    flavors

    grain, tannin, wood, oak, smoky
