    synonyms

    Tequila platino, Tequila plato

    description

    Tequila blanco is a generic category for distilled fermented agave that is aged no more than two months.

    flavors
    herbal, vegetal, smoky, agave
    substitute
    other Tequila blanco, or a Tequila reposado
    origin
    Mexico
