    sloe gin

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored /
    • sloe gin

    description

    Sloe gin is generic for a particular liqueur with natural and/or artificial sloeberry flavoring, with either a grain neutral spirit base or (rarely, and mostly historically) a gin base. The sloeberry is another name for the blackthorn plum.

    flavors
    sloe, fruit, sweet, plum, berries
    substitute
    other plum, prune, or berry liqueur
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Germany, England, Canada, France, United Kingdom
