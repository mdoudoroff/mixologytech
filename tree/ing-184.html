    strawberry-flavored liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored /
    • strawberry-flavored liqueur

    synonyms

    strawberry schnapps, creme de fraise, fraise liqueur

    description

    Strawberry-flavored liqueur is a generic category for liqueur with natural and/or artificial strawberry flavoring in either brandy or neutral spirit base.

    flavors
    berries, sweet, fruit, strawberry
    substitute
    other berry-flavored liqueur (imperfect substitute)
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Netherlands, Canada, Italy, France, Australia, Missouri (United States)
