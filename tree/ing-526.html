    amaro

    • liqueurs /
    • aperitif/digestif bitters /
    • amaro

    description

    Amaro is a generic term for intensely-flavored aperitif/digestif bitters of Italian origin. Similar to the French “amer”. Italian amari are generally higher proof than their French counterparts (with notable exceptions) and most focus on the digestif role, rather the aperitif role (again, with notable exceptions).

    flavors
    herbal, sweet, bitter, medicinal
    substitute
    Other aperitif/digestif bitters. Nearly all aperitif/digestif bitters are complex, proprietary formulas and even products that aim to knock off better known brands tend to noticeably distinct. Factoring the extreme flavor potency of all these liqueurs, none are exactly substitutes for each other, but othen one can be substituted to different effect.
