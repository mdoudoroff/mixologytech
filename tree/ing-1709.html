    Carpano Antica Formula

    • Carpano Antica Formula

    synonyms

    antica formula, carpano antica

    description

    Proprietary vanilla-flavored red vermouth. Carpano Antica Formula is the lone commercial example “vermouth alla vaniglia” today, at least outside of Italy. This is a superb product well suited for combining with aged spirits in mixed drinks where the vanilla is compatible.

    Vermouth alla vaniglia is a venerable Turinese style of red vermouth with added vanilla flavoring.

    Brand: Carpano

    flavors
    fruit, wine, herbal, vanilla, acid, spice
    substitute
    Other red vermouth (you can experiment with adding an iota of vanilla extract or a tiny amount of vanilla liqueur to a conventional red vermouth to compensate for the vanilla flavoring).

    Commentary

    Since its introduction in the 1990s, this fabulous vermouth has rightly won widespread appreciation for its rich, delicious flavor and almost singlehandedly forced many to reevaluate their ideas about vermouth. In turn, the renewed interest has revitalized the category.

    What many people don’t realize is that this vermouth contains a lot of vanilla, which is why it harmonizes so beautifully with many aged spirits, particularly bourbon and rye. Now that we have other high quality options for red vermouths, Antica Formula is not always the “best” choice when vanilla isn’t desirable (e.g., gin drinks).

    Martin D (2012 March 16 10:10AM)
