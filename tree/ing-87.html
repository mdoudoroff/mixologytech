    Jamaica Ginger DEFUNCT

    • non-beverage concentrates /
    • chemical additives /
    • Jamaica Ginger

    synonyms

    ginger essence, Extract of ginger

    description

    Jamaica Ginger is a defunct generic USP grade liquid ginger compound used medicinally as an antispasmodic and in drinks as a ginger flavoring, added in dashes. Prohibition saw Jamaica ginger abused in much larger quantities as an additive to “bathtub gin” and other crudely-distilled and foul-tasting products prepared for scofflaw drinkers. This often led to cases of temporary semi-paralysis, known as “jake-leg”, an ironic variation of the tern “jack-leg”, meaning the stagger around without control.
