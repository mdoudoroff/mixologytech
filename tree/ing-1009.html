    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, herbal or spice-flavored

    description

    Herbal or spice-flavored liqueurs is an abstract category for sweetened and flavored spirits of various dilutions with herb and/or spice flavoring.

    flavors
    sweet
    substitute
    other herbal or spice-flavored liqueur (likely to be difficult to substitute)
