    maraschino liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored /
    • maraschino liqueur

    synonyms

    Marasquin

    description

    Maraschino liqueur is generic for a category of clear liqueur with a neutral spirit base, flavored with crushed Dalmation Marasca cherries & the pits, stems & seeds deriving therefrom. A truly classic cocktail liqueur noted for its fresh flavor. A component in the La Floridita Daiquiri and the Aviation Cocktail.

    flavors
    wood, fruit, grain, cherry, sweet, berries
    substitute
    There is no real substitute for maraschino liqueur. You can replace it with Cointreau, which will result in a different drink but maintain the overall balance.
    places (country/region of production)
    Italy, Eastern Europe, Croatia
