    lime syrup

    • non-alcoholic sweeteners /
    • syrup /
    • syrup, fruit-flavored /
    • lime syrup

    synonyms

    lime cordial

    description

    Lime syrup is a cooked syrup consisting of water, sugar and fresh-squeezed lime juice. Various techniques can be employed for clarification if required. Commercial preparations are widely available, often with added green food coloring.

    sweet
