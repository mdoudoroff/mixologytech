    coffee

    • non-alcoholic mixers & dilutants /
    • non-alcoholic infusions & tisanes /
    • coffee

    description

    Coffee is the beverage derived from seeds (beans), of the plant of the same name, roasted and ground for beverage use and used as a popular mixer in hot and cold mixed drinks. It is rarely used in cocktails per sé, though, in small quantities, it is an excellent mixer in that regard. The beans and grinds themselves are occasionally used as garnish or flavoring and as the basis for liqueurs and syrups.
