    • brands /
    • Jim Beam

    description

    Bourbon distiller and brand that began as a farm distillery in 1795 with Johannes “Reginald” Beam (orig. Boehm). Descendents remain involved in the operation today, notably distiller Fred Noe, part of the 7th generation. The company produces several bourbons and straight ryes under the Jim Beam brand, plus a variety of other bourbons, and straight ryes and blended whiskeys.
