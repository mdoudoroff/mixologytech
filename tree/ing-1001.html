    succulent-based spirits

    • succulent-based spirits

    description

    Succulent-based spirits is an abstract category for spirits distilled from the cooked, fermented hearts of succulent plants. The most common example is mescal, which is produced from varieties of the agave. Effectively all succulent-based spirits originate from Mexico or related areas.
