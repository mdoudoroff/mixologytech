    apple brandy, American

    • brandies /
    • brandy, aged /
    • apple brandy /
    • apple brandy, American

    description

    American apple brandy is generic for American brandy distilled from apples. Often another term for applejack. Marketed as a straight distillate with only water dilution to regulate the proof, or, less frequently, blended with neutral spirits. Excellent in cocktails.

    flavors
    whiskey, brandy, wood, apple, fruit
    substitute
    other apple brandy or other aged brandy.
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Ohio (United States), New York (United States), Oregon (United States)
