Jack Daniels Honey

description

Jack Daniels Tennessee Honey is a proprietary example of Bourbon whiskey-flavored liqueur.

Bourbon whiskey-flavored liqueur is generic for liqueur with Bourbon whiskey flavor/base. Brand examples: Jeremiah Weed, Wild Turkey Bourbon Liqueur.

Due to savvy marketing from the 1940s into the 1970s, Southern Comfort has gained a position as the premier American whiskey-based liqueur. However it was made before Prohibition, unfortunately it does not have a whiskey base today; it is made with grain neutral spirits and flavorings (and may always have been.)
