    crème de noyeau

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, nut or bean-flavored /
    • almond-flavored liqueur /
    • crème de noyeau

    synonyms

    noyeau, creme de noyeaux, creme de noyau, creme de almond

    description

    Crème de noyeau is generic for a liqueur, originally French, with natural and/or artificial almond flavoring and depending on the brand, a neutral spirit or brandy base. Lurid coloring is not uncommon. Associated with mixed drinks such as the Pink Squirrel.

    flavors
    nutty, apricot pits, sweet, almond, spice
    substitute
    amaretto
    places (country/region of production)
    Canada, Netherlands

    examples

    • Bols Creme de Noyaux
    • Hiram Walker Crème de Noyaux
