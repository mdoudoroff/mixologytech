    Old Tom gin

    • neutral spirits & grain eau de vie /
    • gin /
    • Old Tom gin

    synonyms

    Tom gin

    description

    Old Tom gin is a generic category for slightly sweetened column still-produced gin of a very early variety; the “missing link” between Dutch genever or “Holland” gin and the newly-developed London Dry Gin type.

    flavors
    floral, herbal, alcohol, juniper, spice
    substitute
    lightly sweeten a dry gin with simple syrup to produce a rough approximation
    places (country/region of production)
    England (United Kingdom), United States

    examples

    • Booth’s Old Tom Gin
    • Hayman’s Old Tom Gin
    • Ransom Old Tom Gin
