    passion fruit nectar

    • non-alcoholic mixers & dilutants /
    • fruit juice /
    • passion fruit nectar

    description

    Passion fruit nectar is a preparation of the juice and pulp of the South American passion fruit. Used almost exclusively in tropical drinks. In temperate climes, usually purchased frozen. The actual plain juice is rarely seen.

    flavors
    tropical fruit, fruit, passion fruit
    places (country/region of production)
    Indiana (United States), France
