    description

    Proprietary rhum agricole from Martinique.

    Aged Martinique rum is generic for the barrel-aged rum (rhum agricole) of Martinique. Aged rum from Martinique is commonly produced in the 1-2 year amber “élevé sous bois” form and in an older, darker “vieux” form (although the terminology varies from brand to brand).
