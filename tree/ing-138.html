    apricot liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, fruit-flavored /
    • apricot liqueur
    More...

    synonyms

    apricot-flavored brandy, apricot schnapps, apricot cordial

    description

    Apricot liqueur is generic for any brandy or neutral spirit-based liqueur with natural and/or artificial apricot flavoring and an amber color. One of the earliest fruit liqueurs produced.

    Do not confuse apricot liqueurs, particularly “apricot brandy” with apricot eau de vie, which is a true fruit brandy and an utterly dry distillation of fermented apricots.

    flavors
    sweet, fruity, apricot
    substitute
