    Pineau des Charentes

    • fermented & brewed alcoholic ingredients

        • wine, fortified
          • Pineau des Charentes

    description

    Pineau des Charentes is the generic category for a regional traditional blend of Cognac and grape juice (grape must) from Charantes, France. Essentially a mistelle that is aged at least 18 months, and some much longer than that. Not a true aperitif.

    flavors

    brandy, berries, grape, fruity, wine

    origin

    France

    examples

    • Chateau de Montifaud Pineau de Charentes
    • Le Coq d'Or
    • Pineau des Charentes Marnier Grande Reserve
