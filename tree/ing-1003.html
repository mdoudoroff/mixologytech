    description

    Bottled cocktails are an abstract category for alcoholic concoctions that are assembled in advance and packaged for later use. Bottled cocktails are either ready to consume out of their container or are intended to be combined with a “mixer”.

    substitute
    Primary substitute recourse is a self-produced simulacrum.
