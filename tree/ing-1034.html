    hot sauce

    • non-beverage concentrates /
    • prepared condiments /
    • hot sauce

    synonyms

    pepper sauce, chile sauce

    description

    Hot sauce is a generic category for condiment sauces made from chile peppers and other ingredients, usually in a vinegar base. Sauces vary extensively in ingredients, concentration, flavor, color and heat.

    flavors
    piquant, sour, chile pepper, vegetal
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Mexico

    examples

    • Cholula Hot Sauce (Original)
    • Tabasco sauce
