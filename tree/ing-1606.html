    syrup, fruit-flavored

    • non-alcoholic sweeteners /
    • syrup /
    • syrup, fruit-flavored

    description

    Fruit-flavored syrup is an abstract category for sugar syrup infused with the fruit, fruit juices and/or skins of fresh or dried fruit.
