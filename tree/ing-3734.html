    description

    Macallan 1824 Select Oak, The is a proprietary example of Speyside single malt scotch.

    Legally delimited subregion of the Highlands single malt scotch region, and where the highest density of scotch distilleries is located. The chief geographical feature of Speyside is the Spey river, along which many distilleries are located and draw water from. Scotch from Speyside is just that, and connotes no particular style or qualities.
