    non-beverage concentrates

    • non-beverage concentrates

    description

    Non-beverage concentrates is an abstract category for liquids, semi-liquids and dissoluble solids whose primary purpose in mixed drinks is to introduce flavor (or other characteristics) without dramatically diluting the drink. Most of these items crossed to the bar from the kitchen larder.
