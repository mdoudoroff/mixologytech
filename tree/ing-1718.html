    • Lucas Bols

    synonyms

    Bols

    description

    Venerable Dutch genever distiller today primarily associated with its broad range of inexpensive liqueurs, some of which are produced under contract in other markets and may not meet the same quality standards as those produce directly by Bols in the Netherlands.
