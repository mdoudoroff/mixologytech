    Worcestershire sauce

    • non-beverage concentrates /
    • prepared condiments /
    • Worcestershire sauce

    synonyms

    Wooster sauce

    description

    Worcestershire sauce is generic (but formerly proprietary) for a nonalcoholic condiment sauce blending a fermentation of vinegar, molasses, anchovies, tamarind, shallots, garlic, onion, cloves, and chili peppers. The original brand, Lea & Perrin is recommended insofar as no artificial flavors or colors are used. Common in drinks in the Bloody Mary family, among others.
