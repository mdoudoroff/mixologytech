    orange flower water

    • non-alcoholic mixers & dilutants /
    • non-alcoholic infusions & tisanes /
    • orange flower water

    synonyms

    orange blossom water, Fleur d’Orange, orange flower essence

    description

    Orange flower water is generic for an intensely floral aromatic water produced using perfume distillation techniques on orange blossoms. The diluted result is used as a flavoring in cooking and mixed drinks.

    flavors
    floral, orange, fruit, citrus
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Netherlands, Illinois (United States)

    examples

    • Fee Brothers Orange Flower Water
    • Indo-European Orange Blossom Water
    • Nielsen-Massey Orange Blossom Water
