synonyms

100% de agave, 100% puro de agave, 100% blue agave

description

Tequila agave is generic for tequila containing nothing but distilled, fermented blue agave (and water). Tequilas that meet this requirement will state the fact clearly on their label.

herbal, vegetal, smoky, agave
