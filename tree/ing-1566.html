    fennel seed

    • fennel seed

    synonyms

    aniseseed

    description

    Fennel seed is the dried seed of the fennel or anise plants, commonly used as a spice in Mediterranean and Near Eastern cooking, and as a popular flavoring for liquors. Note: fennel and anise are not technically the same plants, but they are treated somewhat interchangeably in common practice.
