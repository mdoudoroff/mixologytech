    parfait amour

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, herbal or spice-flavored /
    • parfait amour

    synonyms

    parfait d'amour

    description

    Parfait amour is generic for a once-popular violet-colored 19th century liqueur of spices, vanilla, orange, and flowers. Notable in the Jupiter Cocktail.

    flavors
    fruit, marshmallow, jellybean, citrus, sweet, vanilla, spice
    substitute
    other herbal or spice-flavored liqueur (likely to be difficult to substitute)
    places (country/region of production)
    Netherlands, Australia, France
