    Progressive Hebridean Distillers

    description

    Independent, venerable scotch whisky distillery on Islay traditionally known for its unpeated single malt; under Jim McEwan has, since 2000, emitted a sprawling, adventuresome product line that largely eschews conventional single malt product segmentation for variety, as well as gin product and the “independent bottling” of rums under its Renegade Rum brand. Please see bottle label for details.
