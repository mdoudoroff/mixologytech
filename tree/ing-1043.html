    almond-flavored liqueur

    • liqueurs /
    • liqueurs, nut or bean-flavored /
    • almond-flavored liqueur

    description

    Almond-flavored liqueur is a generic category for sweetened and flavored spirits (usually neutral spirits) of various dilutions with almond flavoring.

    flavors
    sweet, almond, nutty
    substitute
    other nut- or bean-flavored liqueur
    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Italy, Netherlands, Canada, Kentucky (United States), Germany, Belgium, California (United States)

    sub-branches

    • amaretto (25)
    • crème de noyeau (2)

    examples

    • Niederegger Cuandole Marzipan Sahneliqueur
    • Orendain Crema de Almendrado
