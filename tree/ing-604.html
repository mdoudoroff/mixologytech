    spiced rum

    • sugar cane & molasses-based spirits
      • rum
        • rum, flavored
          • spiced rum

    description

    Spiced rum is a generic category for rum with additive flavors, originally (and still) considered a folk liquor in Jamaica, it has become popular as commercially bottled by companies like Captain Morgan and Bacardi. The prevalent spice in spiced rum is vanilla with other spices in slight evidence, though in folk preparations cinnamon and allspice berry flavors are also common.

    flavors

    vanilla, rum, spice

    substitute

    other spiced rum or homemade: at its simplest, flavor an inexpensive rum (e.g., Jamaican) with a touch of vanilla; more sophisticated spiced rums can be produced by blending with tinctures to taste, or through direct infusion with spices and fruits
