    Hiram Walker

    • brands
      • Hiram Walker

    description

    Venerable Canadian brand dating to 1856 that produced Canadian Club whisky and especially responsible for the success of Canadian Whisky in the US market (ironically, the company was founded and owned by American citizens until 1926). Today, the brand is most directly associated with a wide range of inexpensive flavored liqueurs.
