    description

    Major Peters “The Works” Bloody Mary Mix is a proprietary example of Bloody Mary mix.

    Bloody Mary mix is generic for the commercial, pre-bottled Bloody Mary drink containing tomato juice and other flavoring constituents, lacking only the alcohol. While useful in a pinch, careful preparation of one's own drink from scratch with either tomato juice or V8 juice is preferable. See Bloody Mary in the recipe section for ideas and instructions.
