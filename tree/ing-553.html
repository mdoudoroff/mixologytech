    apple brandy

    • brandies /
    • brandy, aged /
    • apple brandy

    synonyms

    cider brandy

    description

    Apple brandy is generic for the aged distillate of fermented apple cider from any of several countries including France, the United States, Germany or Japan. Notable examples include Calvados and applejack.

    flavors
    brandy, wood, apple, fruit
    substitute
    other apple brandy or other aged brandy.
    places (country/region of production)
    France, United States, Japan, Oregon (United States), Austria, New York (United States), Ohio (United States)

    sub-branches

    • apple brandy, American (11)
    • Calvados (19)
