    description

    Bourbon whiskey is a generic category for U.S. single-malt whiskey distilled from predominately corn mash with wheat or rye grains. Popular in all manner of mixed drinks 19th century-present.

    flavors
    whiskey, wood, grain, corn, oak, tannin
    substitute

    places (country/region of production)
    United States, Kentucky (United States), New York (United States), Virginia (United States), Texas (United States), Ohio (United States), Colorado (United States)
