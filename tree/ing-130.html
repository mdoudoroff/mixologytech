    whipped cream

    • garnishes & solids /
    • whipped cream

    description

    Whipped cream typically refers to an appropriate size pour of sweetened or unsweetened whole cream that has been whipped just to the point of peaking. The commercially pressurized-and-canned variety should generally be avoided unless specified.
