My Bar

My Bar is a premium iPhone app that makes simple inventory management feasible for liquor enthusiasts and mixologists.

Important! My Bar does things that no other app can, but My Bar is not for everyone. Please do not purchase My Bar until you know it will provide you with value.

  1. Download and play with our free app Bar Ingredients, which will introduce you to the MixologyTech Ingredients Database.
  2. Watch the screencast video (below) to understand how My Bar’s features work.
  3. Email me with any questions or doubts you have.

iPad users: this app has no native iPad interface, but it is usable with the iPhone UI on iPad and iPad 2 (including iPad 2 camera support for barcode scanning).

Thank you!

Features

  • All the features of the Bar Ingredients app, plus…
  • Lists: track your inventory, shopping list, favorites, etc. Create your own custom lists for whatever purpose.
  • NEW in 1.7 Custom lists now have an optional type, making it easy to segment your lists. For example, create a custom list named “storage room” of type Inventory and what you track in that list will be clearly represented in your master Inventory list. You can change the type of any custom list with impunity.
  • Barcode scanning: use the built-in camera on your device to look up items and to batch scan into your lists. The spirits database recognizes 5000+ barcodes (more every week through crowd sourcing). Add unrecognized bar codes on the fly.
  • Custom ingredients: add your own entries for limited edition items and things that aren't in the official database yet.
  • Notes: add simple annotations to entries for future reference.
  • iCloud support: all your lists, custom ingredients and notes are automatically backed up and sync’d through iCloud if you’re an iCloud user.
