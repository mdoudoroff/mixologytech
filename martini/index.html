Here we focus entirely on the king of cocktails, the Martini, and explore it exhaustively.

  • No citrus drinks
  • No “chocolate martinis” or nonsense of that kind
  • Nothing actually sweet—dry, and off-dry drinks only
  • Gin cocktails (73%) and vodka cocktails (13%), nearly all with vermouth or other aromatic wines (see full list of included recipes)
  • A hundred carefully-curated recipes* spanning the entire history of the Martini, beginning with its first documented origins in the 1880s and running to present day interpretations
  • Over 70 Dry Martinis (and close relatives) to explore, drawn from essential books, famous personalities, and institutions
  • Over 30 off-dry Martinis (and close relatives) to rediscover employing red vermouth, white vermouth, genever, and/or Old Tom Gin
  • Fully-annotated by author and journalist Robert Simonson
  • The perfect companion to Simonson’s The Martini Cocktail: A Meditation on the World's Greatest Drink, with Recipes, available from Ten Speed Press
  • Check off the ingredients you’ve got and this app will show you which drinks you can make!
  • Filter the recipes on multiple criteria with just a few taps
  • Track favorite and flagged recipes
  • Ingredient definitions included
  • Universal app, works nearly identically on both platforms

*Note that 49 of the recipes in this app can also be found in our app Martin’s Index; in this Martini app, they may be annotated differently.
