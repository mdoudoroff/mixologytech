My Bar 1.5

February 7, 2012

Major new features:

  • Quantity tracking for inventory lists, including batch scanning options for incrementing/decrementing quantities
  • Notes field for adding your own annotations to ingredients
  • iCloud support for inventory lists, notes and custom ingredients
  • Commentary about ingredients from the users
  • Streamlined batch scanning and handling for unrecognized bar codes
  • Patch updates to the database are now automatically applied on launch
  • Myriad fixes and minor improvements
